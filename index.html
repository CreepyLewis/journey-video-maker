🎬 Journey Video Maker

Create TikTok / Reels-style cartoon journey videos right in your browser — no software, no account. Inspired by adventure creators like Motu Patlu Journey 🏍️

🚀 Quick Start (30 seconds)

  1. Click 🎬 OPEN STUDIO above
  2. Type your video title & handle
  3. Pick a cartoon theme (🏍️ Moto is the Motu Patlu style)
  4. Add scenes — each scene gets a caption + location
  5. Click ▶ Animate to preview
  6. Click 🎬 RENDER VIDEO to create your WebM video
  7. Download and post to TikTok / Reels!

📹 Output Format

Videos are exported as WebM (VP9) — the best web video format. TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts all accept WebM. You can also convert to MP4 using cloudconvert.com for free.

📲 Works on Mobile

Open in Chrome or Safari on Android/iPhone. Preview and edit scenes on your phone. Rendering may be slow on older devices — use a PC for best results.

🛠️ Tech Stack

  • HTML5 Canvas API — all cartoon drawing (no external libraries)
  • MediaRecorder API — captures canvas frames as video
  • Zero dependencies — single HTML file, works offline after first load

🏍️ About the Motu Patlu Style

The default cartoon character is drawn in the Motu Patlu style — a popular Indian cartoon known for its fat, round characters with huge heads, simple expressions, and colourful outfits. The biker character bounces rhythmically, has a big grin, and rides a chunky cartoon motorcycle with spinning wheels.
